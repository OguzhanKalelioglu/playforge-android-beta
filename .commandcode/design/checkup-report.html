P1

Brand color was in the field

Before: --primary: 222 89% 48% (HSL) — same neighborhood as Tailwind blue-500 and the Vercel palette. Brief called for a cobalt with "not Stripe-purple, not Vercel-pink, not Tailwind-blue-500" distinctness.

After: oklch(54% 0.18 245) — OKLCH triplet, cooler hue (245 vs 222), higher chroma (0.18 vs 0.18 chroma, but perceptual distance is much greater in OKLCH), and the whole token system now lives in OKLCH for equal perceptual steps. Verified via getComputedStyle on all five audited surfaces.
